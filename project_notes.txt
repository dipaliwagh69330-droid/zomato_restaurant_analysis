project title

Zomato Restaurant Analytics & Customer Insights

project objective

this project analyzes Zomato restaurant data to understand customer preferences, restaurant performance, pricing trends, and online ordering behavior. The goal is to discover business insights that can improve customer engagement and restaurant operations.

Dataset Information

Dataset Name: Zomato Restaurant Dataset

Total Rows: 7105

Columns:
restaurant name
restaurant type
rate (out of 5)
num of ratings
avg cost (two people)
online order
table booking
cuisines type
area
local address

Business Questions

1. Which restaurant types receive the highest ratings?
2. Which areas contain the highest number of restaurants?
3. Which cuisines are most popular among customers?
4. Does online ordering impact restaurant ratings?
5. Which restaurants have the highest customer engagement based on number of ratings?
6. What is the relationship between average cost and restaurant ratings?
7. Which areas contain the most expensive restaurants?
8. Does table booking affect restaurant ratings?
9. Which cuisines appear most frequently across restaurants?
10. Which restaurant types are most common in different areas?


Expected Insights

- Identify highly rated restaurant categories
- Discover customer ordering preferences
- Understand pricing trends across locations
- Analyze factors affecting restaurant ratings
- Identify areas with strong restaurant competition
